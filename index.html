This is a clone of the classic arcade game frogger. How To Play:

  1. Use the arrow keys to move or the controller if you are playing on a smartphone or a tablet.
  2. To win you need to reach the water.
  3. Avoid the enemies(bugs).
  4. Collect gems to increase your score.
  5. The rock is impassable.
  6. The game has 2 levels. once you finish the first one, the second starts immediately.
  7. You can change your player avatar from the list on the left.
